Frequently Asked Questions

What makes D. Durga different from other soft skills trainers?
D. Durga holds five degrees — DPN, DAHM, BBA, MBA, and MSW in Medical & Psychiatry. No other soft skills trainer in Tamil Nadu combines clinical psychology, HR management, hospital administration, and marketing. Every workshop is evidence-based and psychologically grounded.
Are workshops available online for clients outside Chennai?
Yes. All programs are available online via Zoom or Google Meet — for individuals, corporate teams, hospitals, and colleges across India and for NRI clients internationally. Tamil and English sessions available.
Can you conduct workshops at our hospital or college?
Absolutely. On-site workshops are available across Tamil Nadu for hospitals, nursing colleges, engineering colleges, corporate offices, and institutions. Programs are customised for your specific audience — from 10 to 500+ participants.
